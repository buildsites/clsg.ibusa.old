About

CLSG, Ibusa is a Chapter of CLSG National. CLSG National is a group of well meaning Nigerians who are interested in Good, Credible, Capable, Competent, Innovative, Creative, and Transparent Leaders in all arms of governance in Nigeria. CLSG Ibusa is a vibrant chapter of CLSG and is determined to see that good governance is entrenched in Ibusa and her environs.

Mission

  • To ensure the 2023 election is free and fair across the Nation particularly in Ibusa, Delta State.
  • To massively mobilize Ibusa residents to be involved in the process of producing credible, competent, transparent, and committed leaders particularly at the Presidential level and in Ibusa, Delta State.
  • To collaborate with other support groups and like-minded individuals to realize these goals.

Vision

To source and enthrone credible, and competent leaders with verifiable track record of success,who have the capacity, capability, boldness, courage and passion to deliver the dividend of democracy to the Nigerian people particularly in Ibusa, Delta State.
