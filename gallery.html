About Credible Leadership

CLSG, Ibusa is a Chapter of CLSG National. CLSG National is a group of well meaning Nigerians who are interested in Good, Credibl, Capable, Competence, Innovative, Creative, and Transparent Leaders in all arms of governance in Nigeria. CLSG Ibusa is a vibrant chapter of CLSG and is determined to see that good governance is entrenched in Ibusa and her environs
